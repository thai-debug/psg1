Khamkeut Saen Oudom Limited

The Khamkeut Saen-Udom (KSO) Gold Mine Project is located amidst mountainous terrain along the Vietnamese and Lao border, about 22 KMs from the town of Lak Sao and about 450 KMs south of Vientiane Capital.

The concession area spans over 1,802 sq KMs. Phonesack Group has spent a major portion of its resources to develop this project by using internationally recognized consultants and systems.
